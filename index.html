Questions

Before you ask.

Do I need to be advanced?

No — most clients start somewhere between "can kick up" and "can’t hold it yet." Complete beginners are welcome; the programme starts from wherever you are.

Can I do this online?

Yes. Programming and video form-checks work anywhere — you film your drills, I review and correct. In-person sessions at OverGravity are an optional add-on for London folk.

What if I’m injured or have dodgy wrists?

Tell me at the audit. We build around it — wrist prep and load management are baked into every programme, and Beth (Chartered Physiotherapist) is on the team.

How do I start?

Join The Coaching Programme (£100/mo online, or £150/mo with a monthly in-person session) and book your onboarding call. Prefer to try first? A one-off 1-2-1 is £80, credited to your first month if you join within 14 days. Unsure? Book a free 15-min call.
