Who It's For

Anyone serious about inverting.

If you're willing to put in the work — there's a level of 1-1 attention here that group classes simply can't match.

🌱

Beginners

Learn with correct technique from day one. No bad habits, no months of confusion. Build the foundation properly — wrists, shoulders, alignment, entry.

📊

Intermediates who've plateaued

Can kick up but can't balance consistently? This is exactly where 1-1 makes the difference. We find the specific gap and fix it.

⚡

Advanced students

Refining presses, shapes, one-arm foundations, or advanced line work. Precise coaching at this level requires specific eyes on your specific movement.
