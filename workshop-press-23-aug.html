Is this you?

Who it's for

This one has a strength floor. It's built for people who already hold a handstand and now want to press up into it — with control, from the floor, no jump. If that's you, this is the session that cracks it.

✓ You hold a handstand

A 10-second freestanding handstand, or a solid 30 seconds chest-to-wall. You've got the top position — now you want to build into it from the floor.

✓ You keep jumping it

You can muscle a press with a little hop, but you want the real thing: slow, controlled, hips leading, no momentum.

Not quite for you if…

You can't yet hold a 10-second handstand or 30 seconds chest-to-wall. The press needs that base first — do our beginner workshop or the free app, then come back for this.
