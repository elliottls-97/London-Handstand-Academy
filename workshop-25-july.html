Is this you?

Who it's for

Open to everyone from total beginners to those trying to hold their handstand for longer. In this fun, dynamic session we cover entering and exiting safely, working with and without a spotter, and even handstand walks.

✓ Complete beginners

No previous experience required. If you've never been upside down in your life, you're in the right place — we start from the ground up.

✓ Chasing a consistent hold

Already kicking up but want to break the 10–15 second mark reliably instead of catching it for a lucky second.

✓ Anyone who moves

Gym, yoga, calisthenics, climbing or curious first-timer — we work with whatever you bring and build the rest.

Not quite for you if…

You already hold a solid freestanding handstand and want advanced press or one-arm work — that's our 1-1 coaching, not this class.

From past workshops

Questions

I've literally never done a handstand — can I come?

If you can get into a kick-up against a wall, yes. If you're starting from complete zero, you'll still get plenty out of it, but message me first so I know to expect you.

What if I'm not flexible or strong?

You don't need to be either. We work with what you've got and show you how to build the rest. Handstands are a skill, not a party trick for the already-bendy.

Where exactly is it, and how do I get there?

OverGravity Gymnastics in Shadwell, East London — map's above. Shadwell station (Overground & DLR) is the closest; Wapping is also walkable. Full address and directions come in your booking confirmation.

What should I wear / bring?

Comfortable workout kit you can move in, water, and either bare feet or grippy socks. That's it.

What happens after the workshop?

You leave with a take-home plan and free access to our Handstand Ladder app. If you want to keep progressing with structure, there's monthly coaching — but there's zero pressure on the day.
